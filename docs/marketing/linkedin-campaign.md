## Lead Magnet

### "The Australian Tradesperson's Guide to IICRC Certification"

**Format:** PDF, 12–16 pages, professionally designed with CARSI branding.

**Table of Contents:**

1. **What is IICRC?** — International Institute of Inspection, Cleaning and Restoration Certification explained for the Australian market
2. **Why IICRC Matters in Australia** — Insurance requirements, tender pre-qualification, WHS compliance links
3. **The 5 Core IICRC Disciplines** — WRT, CRT/CCT, OCT, ASD, AMRT — what each covers and who needs it
4. **CECs Explained** — How Continuing Education Credits work, renewal cycles, how to track them
5. **Industry Applications** — How IICRC training applies to Restoration, Mining, Aged Care, Property, Cleaning, Construction
6. **Online vs Classroom Training** — Pros, cons, and why online delivery suits Australian tradespeople
7. **How to Get Started** — Step-by-step guide to choosing your first course
8. **CARSI Course Catalogue Overview** — Summary of 40+ available courses with CEC values
9. **Group Training & Corporate Licensing** — Options for businesses enrolling teams
10. **FAQs** — Common questions from Australian professionals considering IICRC training

**CTA on final page:** "Ready to start? Visit carsi.com.au or contact us at training@carsi.com.au"

### Privacy & Consent

- Privacy policy URL: carsi.com.au/privacy
- Consent text: "By submitting this form, you agree to receive training information from CARSI. You can unsubscribe at any time."
- Custom thank-you message: "Thanks! Check your inbox for the IICRC Certification Guide. A CARSI training adviser will be in touch within 2 business days."

### Lead Routing

1. Form submission → CRM (or spreadsheet initially)
2. Automated email: deliver PDF lead magnet immediately
3. Within 2 business days: personal follow-up email from CARSI training adviser
4. Day 7: follow-up email with relevant course recommendation based on Industry field
5. Day 14: final follow-up with group pricing if Team Size > 1

## Retargeting Strategy

### Audience Build

| Audience                              | Source                                 | Window  |
| ------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- | ------- |
| Website visitors                      | LinkedIn Insight Tag on carsi.com.au   | 90 days |
| Course page viewers                   | URL contains /courses/ or /industries/ | 60 days |
| Lead gen form openers (not submitted) | LinkedIn engagement                    | 30 days |
| Video viewers (50%+)                  | LinkedIn video ads (future)            | 90 days |

### Sequential Ad Strategy

| Stage            | Audience                        | Ad Type                    | Message                                 |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------- | -------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| 1. Awareness     | Cold audience (targeting above) | Single Image + Document Ad | Problem/risk hook + lead magnet         |
| 2. Consideration | Website visitors + form openers | Single Image               | Course-specific ad with pricing         |
| 3. Decision      | Course page viewers             | Single Image               | Testimonial/social proof + enrol CTA    |
| 4. Re-engagement | Leads not converted (30+ days)  | Document Ad                | New content piece or limited-time offer |

### Retargeting Budget

Allocate 15–20% of total LinkedIn budget to retargeting ($375–$500/month at $2,500 total).
